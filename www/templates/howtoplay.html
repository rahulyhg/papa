HOW TO PLAY

Unlock new decorations and elements for Bappa every day and keep sharing your creations with your friends.

The more likes you receive from your friends, the more points you collect.

To collect more points daily, perform an Aarti to Ganesha. The mahurat for Aarti is pre-set by the app and lasts for an hour. You will get an alert twice a day.

The contest remains open till 6 pm daily. Users who get maximum likes on a particular day will be declared the winner after 6 pm daily on Facebook. Winners of the 3rd, 5th and 10th day will be featured on TOI page after the Visarjan day.
